# Streamer & Influencer Market Expansion Strategy

## Executive Summary

TrackRights should expand to serve streamers and influencers. This represents a significant market opportunity with similar pain points to music professionals.

## Market Opportunity

### Why This Makes Sense

1. **Large Market**: Millions of streamers/influencers globally
2. **High Contract Volume**: They sign multiple contracts monthly (sponsorships, platform deals, collaborations)
3. **Similar Pain Points**: Complex legal language, need for protection, lack of legal knowledge
4. **Growing Industry**: Creator economy is expanding rapidly
5. **Marketing Advantage**: Creators have built-in audiences for organic growth

### Market Size
- **Twitch**: 7+ million active streamers
- **YouTube**: 50+ million creators
- **TikTok**: Millions of content creators
- **Instagram**: 500K+ influencers
- **Total Addressable Market**: 100M+ creators globally
